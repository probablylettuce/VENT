How to use VENT NY

There are two ways to use this space.


VENT:

Post a frustration, irritation or injustice here. This space is built for words.


COMMENT:

Paste a web link in response to something in a VENT's thread. This is your comment.

Use images, gifs and articles to add your knowledge, support or wit to the implicit conversation.


The purpose of this feature is to challenge the standard mode of online discourse. How can a prefabricated link speak effectively for you?
